Barfi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milkpowder
  • Milk

Recipe

  1. Make Mixture: Mix 2 cups milk powder, 1 cup sugar, and 1/2 cup milk.
  2. Cook: Stir continuously on medium heat until thick and smooth.
  3. Set: Pour mixture into a greased tray; spread evenly.
  4. Garnish: Top with chopped nuts; cool, then cut into squares.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Make Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, ghee, and milk; rest.
  2. Shape Balls: Form small, smooth, crack-free balls.
  3. Fry Balls: Fry until golden brown; drain excess oil.
  4. Make Syrup: Boil sugar, water, cardamom until sticky; soak balls.

Samosa

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Spices
  • All-purpose flour

Recipe

  1. Make Dough: Mix flour, water, oil, and salt; knead into a firm dough.
  2. Prepare Filling: Cook mashed potatoes with peas, cumin, coriander, and spices..
  3. Shape Samosas: Roll dough into small circles, fill with mixture,fold into triangles.
  4. Fry Samosas: Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.

Motichoor Ladoo

Ingredients

  • Gram flour (besan)
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Make Batter: Mix gram flour with water to form a smooth batter.
  2. Fry Boondis: Fry batter through a slotted spoon in hot ghee to make tiny balls.
  3. Make Syrup: Boil sugar with water until sticky; add cardamom.
  4. Combine and Shape: Mix boondis with syrup, cool slightly, then shape into laddus.
